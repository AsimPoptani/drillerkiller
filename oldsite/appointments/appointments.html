Appointments

You can call our surgery for an appointment or use our online appointment request facility instead.

Please note, any NHS treatment is by prior appointment only.

Emergencies

We will endeavor to see dental emergencies on the same day. Please call early to avoid disappointment.

Appointment Cancellations

If you are unable to attend your appointment, please give us at least 24 hours notice. Please note that there may be a charge for late cancellations or missed appointments.

Out Of Hours Information

For out of hours dental services, contact NHS Direct or 0208 867 1411.
