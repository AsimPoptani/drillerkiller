Why DrillerKiller?

In the early 1990's Jeremy Beadle was filming his series of "Beadles About". One of his scripts was about a temporary dental assistant / helper who was set up by Beadle's team.

The production team approached me to film this episode at my surgery and this is what happened. I provided the dental technical advice for this gag.

The episode was dubbed "DRILLER KILLER"

 

When I set up my domain the choice was obvious "DRILLER KILLLER". I have been known as the "DRILLER KILLER" ever since!
